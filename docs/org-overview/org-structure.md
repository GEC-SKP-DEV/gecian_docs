
## Suggested Organization Structure (Flexible)

There is **no fixed structure**. You can keep it **as simple or as detailed as needed**. However, having the following roles is **recommended**:

---

### 1. Lead

* Main person responsible for decisions and direction.
* Manages the team and overall progress.
* Final point of responsibility.

---

### 2. Vice Lead

* Supports the Lead.
* Takes over if the Lead is unavailable.
* Helps coordinate between different roles.

---

### 3. Mentor (Last Year’s Lead)

* The **previous year’s Lead** acts as a **Mentor**.
* Contact the Mentor **only in urgent situations**.
* The Mentor is **not part of official groups or daily operations**.
* Role is advisory only.

---

### 4. Design Lead

* Official lead for **all website design work**.
* Maintains UI/UX consistency.
* Reviews design changes before implementation.

---

### 5. Tech Lead

* Someone **strong in technical skills**.
* Responsible for:

  * Code quality
  * Architecture decisions
  * Technical guidance
* Helps with deployments and tech-related issues.

---

### 6. Outreach Lead

* Handles **outreach, communication, and recruitment**.
* Responsible for:

  * Recruiting **new students** to contribute
  * Growing the **student contributor community**
  * Communicating opportunities to students
* Works on:

  * College outreach
  * Contributor onboarding
  * Increasing participation and engagement

> **Note:** This role does **not focus on social media management**. The main goal is **bringing students into the community and helping them start contributing**.
