## **1. Team Formation Authority**

* The **newly appointed Head (Project Lead)** shall have the authority to **select the rest of the core team** based on project needs.
* Team selection must prioritize **commitment, reliability, and contribution** over popularity or titles.

---

## **2. Criteria for Selecting the New Head**

The current Head shall select the next Head based on the following criteria:

### **a. Technical Competence**

* The candidate must have a **strong understanding of technology** relevant to the project.
* They should be capable of understanding technical discussions and making informed decisions.

---

### **b. Team Leadership Ability**

* The candidate must be able to **motivate, coordinate, and bring others together** to work toward a common goal.
* The ability to **encourage participation and resolve conflicts** is essential.

---

### **c. Responsibility & Execution (Most Important)**

* The candidate must demonstrate that **when responsibility is given, the task is completed**.
* Talent and social skills are valuable, but **without responsibility and follow-through, they are meaningless**.
* **Consistency and reliability** are the most critical qualities for this role.

---

### **d. Commitment & Availability**

* The candidate must be willing to **invest time and effort** regularly.
* Leadership requires presence, follow-ups, and accountability.

---

### **e. Communication Skills**

* The candidate should be able to **communicate clearly and respectfully** with team members, advisors, and administrators.
* Both technical and non-technical communication are important.

---

### **f. Problem-Solving Mindset**

* The candidate should remain calm during challenges and focus on **solutions rather than blame**.
* The ability to make decisions under pressure is essential.

---

### **g. Integrity & Fairness**

* The candidate must act with **honesty, transparency, and fairness**.
* Decisions should always be made in the **best interest of the project**, not personal preferences.

---

### **h. Willingness to Learn**

* The candidate does not need to know everything but must be **open to learning**, feedback, and guidance from seniors and advisors.

---

## üß≠ **Final Principle**

A good leader is not defined by popularity or talent alone, but by **responsibility, consistency, and the ability to get work done through people**.
